# Intro
Infrastructure makes or breaks an engagement, you could have the most evasive loader, the best phishing pretext but none of that matters if your beacon calls out to a 2 day old domain hosted with an IP in a sketchy ASN and a single new firewall rules destorys the engagement before it even began
